  • TSE-21

    • The TSE 21 is the first electric car designed by Team Srijan for the 2020-21 season. The team goal is to develop a single motor-propelled, space frame car weighing 205-215 kg, featuring tunable brake bias, double wishbone suspension, chassis stiffness between 2250-2500 Nm/Deg, and neutral steer handling. The vehicle should be reliable for 260-300 km of rigorous testing and aim to score 450-570 points in dynamic events. The design will be validated using appropriate sensors and detailed documentation for knowledge transfer.

    • Top Speed 95kmph
      Weight 280kg
